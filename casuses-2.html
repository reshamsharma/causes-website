Our Mission: To help women protect themselves against violence by equipping them with personal safety products, information, and awareness

Some cold, hard facts

- 56,0000 maternal deaths before 2010

- 44.5% girls are married before they are 18 years old

- Women in india are sold as chattels, married off and burned, and abused

- A woman in India is raped every 20 minutes

- The market for safety products is fragmented

What We're Doing about It

- We equip women with affordable self-defense tools to protec themselves

- We educate women on how to use these products to stay alert and away from danger

- Contributing to a community based around safety and an eagerness to help one another

- Trying to support open conversations with boys and men to bring about a fundamental shift in how violence against women is perceived
